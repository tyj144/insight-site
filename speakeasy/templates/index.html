What can it do?

Language barriers are unfortunate, but they are avoidable. Why should a capable person be limited by constructs of syntax and structure? SpeakEasy empowers individuals and encourages community-building by assisting in overcoming these limitations.

SpeakEasy is the auto-correct for soft-skills. Based on what you say (word recognition) and the way you say it (facial recognition), SpeakEasy offers pointers on areas of communication users should focus on, either in formal or informal settings.

Whether it be a speech, a class presentation, a business interview, or asking someone on a date, SpeakEasy conforms to the situation and will assist you.

How does it work?

SpeakEasy first takes in a user-recorded video. It analyzes both the audio and the visual components by performing various cognitive processes on them to track facial expressions, emotional changes, and speech selection over time.

SpeakEasy employs Google's speech-to-text API and natural language toolkit for text and Micorosoft's Cognitive Services API for emotional analysis.
